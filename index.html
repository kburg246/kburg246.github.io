~Magnificently Mixed

My name is Kayla, I am a junior-soon-to-be-senior Theatre major at Bob Jones University. I'm a third culture kid who grew up on the mission field on the carribean island of Puerto Rico. Being a missionary kid is hard, because you feel so mixed. I'm too Puerto Rican to be American, and too American to be fully Puerto Rican. But, I get the best of both worlds because since we are a territory, we're automatically U.S. citizens, so there's no issue with visas. Where I am from is a big part of my life, and while I may not be the most traditional puerto rican, that's what unites us, we're all different, but unite on the same core things. An affectionate culture where our friends are our family, a casual way of talking that's normal for us but not for others, and most importantly, working hard, working together, and loving our country.
